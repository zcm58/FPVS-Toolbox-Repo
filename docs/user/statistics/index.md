## Terms used in this section

- **BCA** means baseline-corrected amplitude.
- **ROI** means region of interest, usually a named electrode group.
- **Harmonic** means a multiple of the oddball frequency.
- **Summed BCA** means BCA summed across selected harmonics and averaged within an ROI.
