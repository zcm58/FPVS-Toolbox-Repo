# Glossary

## BCA (Baseline-Corrected Amplitude)

Amplitude at a target frequency minus a local surrounding noise estimate.

## Base frequency

Main visual stimulation rate (Hz), such as 6 Hz.

## Condition

Experimental label mapped to a trigger code in your task.

## FFT

Fast Fourier Transform. Converts time-domain EEG into frequency-domain values.

## Harmonic

Integer multiple of a base frequency (for oddball analysis: 1.2, 2.4, 3.6 Hz, etc.).

## Oddball frequency

Frequency of rare target events embedded in the stimulation stream.

## ROI (Region of Interest)

Named electrode group used for aggregation and statistical testing.

## RM-ANOVA

Repeated-measures ANOVA for testing within-subject effects.

## SNR (Signal-to-Noise Ratio)

Target-bin amplitude divided by local surrounding noise estimate.

## Summed BCA

Primary dependent variable in the stats workflow: BCA summed across selected harmonics and averaged across ROI electrodes.

## Z score

Standardized measure of how far target amplitude is above local noise in standard deviation units.
